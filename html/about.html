KeKunst
An art installation by Kek Kekesen

KeKunst is a revolutionary and bold project made by the renowned and accomplished artist Kek Kekesen. Known for his astonishing, innovative and profound artistic work on Art Noveau and expressionism in paper plane folding, Kek sought out for a new challenge. He decided to share his unimaginable and outstanding mind through an interactive web-installation.

Experience the genius of Kek Kekesen first hand through his expressions of web-art by clicking the bubbles, and feel enlightenment in its corporeal form as Kek's enthralling visions flow through your mind.
